🗣️ Activity 2: Pronunciation Time!

Pronunciation of the '-s' ending

When we add '-s' for the 3rd person (He/She/It), the sound changes depending on the last sound of the verb:

/s/

After voiceless sounds (p, t, k, f)

/z/

After voiced sounds (b, d, g, v, m, n, l, vowels)

/ɪz/

After sibilant sounds (s, z, sh, ch, ge)

📚 Activity 3: Practice Time!

Task 1: Copy the rule in your notebook

Rules for 3rd Person Singular:

  • General rule: Add -s (He runs)
  • Verbs ending in o, ch, sh, ss, x: Add -es (She watches)
  • Consonant + y: Change 'y' to 'i' and add -es (He flies)
  • Have changes to has
